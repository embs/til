> One and only thing I know: I do not know anything.

This could be linked to related modern wordings:
* Maintain a beginner's mind;
* Keep foolish;

Huge benefits arise when we look things like we'd never looked to them before --
or to these kind of thing. We may ask dumb questions that enlight the path to
knowledge and wisdom.

This has a lot to do with not acting automatically. We shall think life and live
according to our thinking.
